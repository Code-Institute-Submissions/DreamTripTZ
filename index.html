Ras Kutani

Ras Kutani is a small, intimate lodge located about 35 kilometers south of Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. It offers a serene and private setting with beautiful views of the Indian Ocean and a lagoon. The lodge features 9 cottages and 4 suites, all made from natural materials like wood and bamboo. Guests can enjoy a variety of activities such as swimming, kayaking, snorkeling, surfing, deep-sea fishing, and horseback riding. The lodge also has a spa where guests can choose from different massages and body treatments.

Udzungwa Mountains National Park (biodiversity hotspot)

Udzungwa Mountains National Park is a biodiversity hotspot located in southern Tanzania. It is known for its rich and unique biodiversity, including a variety of rare, endangered, and endemic species. The park protects important water catchments that supply over 700,000 people and power two hydroelectric plants. Visitors can enjoy hiking, trekking, and birdwatching, with over 400 bird species and 2,500 plant species, many of which are endemic to the area.
